Ultimate Frisbee

Ultimate frisbee is a sport I have been playing for the last 8ish years of my life. My dad played in college and still plays pickup for fun, he would always bring my siblings and I to pickup games and over time I've fallen in love with the sport. Currently, I play for SAAS varsity team and pickup for fun when I can. \

Rules of the Sport

  • The field is 70 yards by 40 commonly played on a soccer field
  • It is a no contact sport with no referes so you have to call fouls and employ the spirit of the game
  • It is commonly played 7 on 7 but if there are not enough people its played 6 v 6 or 5v5
  • You score by catching the disk in the opposing endzone
  • you can't run with the disk in your hands and have to throw to your teammates.
